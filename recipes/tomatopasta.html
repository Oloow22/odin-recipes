Classic tomato pasta recipe

Description

A simple and flavourful pasta dish with a garlic infused tomato sauce topped with fresh basil and Parmesan cheese

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic cloves minced
  • Diced tomatoes
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta then drain and set aside
  2. Heat the olive oil to medium level and add the minced garlic then cook for 1 minute
  3. Add the diced tomatoes and season with salt and pepper
  4. Simmer the tomato sauce for about 10 minutes , stirrig occasionally
  5. Add the cooked pasta to the pan
  6. Serve the pasta hot and garnish with the basil leaves
