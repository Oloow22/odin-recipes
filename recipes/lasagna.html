Lasagna

Description

An easy lasagna recipe that saves me lots of time and serves the whole family

Making good homemade lasagna does not have to be tideous. This lasagna recipe comes together quickly with simple ingredients !

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Spaghetti sauce
  • Cheese : Cottage cheese , Mozzarella & Parmesan
  • Eggs
  • Seasonings : black pepper & salt
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Water

Steps

  1. Cook and drain the ground beef, then stir in the Spaghetti sauce and simmer
  2. Combine the cottage cheese , 2 cups of Mozzarella, eggs , half of the Parmesan and Seasonings
  3. Assemble the lasagna according to the detailed recipe
  4. Bake covered for 45 minutes
  5. Uncover and continue baking for 10 minutes
