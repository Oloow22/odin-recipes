Vegetable stir-fry recipe

Description

A quick and nutritious meal made with a variety of Vegetables seasoned with soy sauce and oyster sauce and served over rice

Ingredients

  • Vegetable oil
  • Sliced onions
  • 1 sliced bell pepper
  • 1 carrot
  • Broccoli
  • Snap peas
  • Soy sauce
  • Oyster sauce
  • Cooked rice

Steps

  1. Heat the Vegetables in a large pan
  2. Add sliced onions and stir for 2 mins
  3. Add bell pepper, carrot, Broccoli & snap peas to the pan then stir for 7 minutes
  4. Add soy sauce and oyster sauce to the pan
  5. Continue to cook for another minute then remove and serve over the cooked rice
